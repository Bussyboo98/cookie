ABOUT

OUR STORE

Caroline's Cookies is a locally owned cookie shop in Lafayette Louisiana that creates hand made, seasonal, and thick & gooey cookies! We have 4 staple flavors that are always on our menu and 2 special monthly flavors which change from month to month. We also offer cookie cakes, catering, and delivery!

Caroline's Cookies owner, Caroline Merryman, started selling her cookies through DM's on our Instagram, then at farmer's markets and pop-ups, then through online orders on our website, and then opened a physical store on August 14, 2021. Since then, we have had to triple our staff and create 2 additional kitchens - all because of the tremendous support of our customers!

We're always thinking of the next fun thing to do: unique monthly flavors, themed events, social media giveaways, collaborations with other local businesses, or bringing back fan favorite flavors! We hope that you'll visit us and try our thick & gooey cookies!

CAROLINE

Caroline Merryman discovered her love for baking at 8 years old by teaching herself how to make cakes and cupcakes. During the COVID lockdown, she started baking lots of cookies and delivering them to her friends as a way to stay busy and not get bored during lockdown. She started an Instagram page for her cookies just for fun, but was soon receiving requests from people to buy them.

She continued to sell her cookies while attending college, until April of 2021 when she decided to put college on hold and focus on giving 100% to Caroline's Cookies. Her dedication paid off with daily lines wrapping around the building of her store as well as selling out of cookies every day.
